Rules

Objective:

Save the world before 125 people die

Backround info:

  • You can vaccinate people by clicking on the green circles (aka people)
  • Each circle represents a person
  • Each second in real life represents a day in the game

How long does each ability lasts:

  • Vaccine: 25 days
  • Immunity: 12 days
  • Infection: spreads every 10 days

Each day:

The game begins with a bunch of green dots on a canvas, two of which are red. The red dots are infected, and every 10 days the disease spreads from the red circle to its neighbors. Keep in mind that in order to be infected the person needs to be right next to the red circle. Throughout the game you will see that the people are of various colors, that is because each color represents a status. In total there are 5 statuses, and 5 different colors. Here is what each color represents:

  • Red: Sick
  • Green: Healthy
  • Yellow: Immune
  • Blue: Vaccinated
  • Black: dead

You will notice that on the right side of the canvas there are numbers, those numbers show how many sick, healthy, immune, vaccinated, and dead people there are.
